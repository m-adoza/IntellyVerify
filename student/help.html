Frequently Asked Questions
How do I submit a project topic?
Click on "Submit Topic" from the sidebar. Fill in the title, problem statement, objectives, and keywords. Then click "Submit Topic". Your submission will be sent to your supervisor for review.
How does plagiarism detection work?
IntelliVerify uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) and Cosine Similarity to compare your topic against all existing topics in the system. A similarity percentage is calculated and classified as Low (0-30%), Medium (31-60%), or High (61-100%) risk.
How can I check the status of my submission?
Go to "Approval Status" from the sidebar. You'll see a list of all your submissions with their current status (Draft, Submitted, Under Review, Approved, Rejected). Click "Details" for more information.
Who can I contact for help?
You can send a message to your supervisor or an administrator through the "Messages" page. Alternatively, you can email support@intelliverify.edu or call +234 706 214 1728.
What happens if my topic is rejected?
If your topic is rejected, you'll receive a notification with feedback from your supervisor or administrator. You can submit a new topic after addressing the feedback.
How do I change my password?
Go to "Profile" from the sidebar. Scroll down to the "Change Password" section, enter your current password and your new password, then click "Change Password".
Can I edit my submitted topic?
Once a topic is submitted, it cannot be edited directly. However, if your supervisor requests revisions, you can submit a new version. For changes, please contact your supervisor.
